Select the class that matches the box within the image. Unclassifiable images, such as those obscured by clouds should be rejected. You may need to pan the map for additional context.

Definitions

Cropland

All cultivated plants harvested for food, feed, and fiber, including plantations (e.g., orchards, vineyards, coffee, tea, rubber).

Pure

Greater than 80% of the pixels in the red box are cropland.

Mixed

Less than 80% and more than 20% of the pixels in the red box are cropland.
